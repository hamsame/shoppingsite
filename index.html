About Us



Our Founders James Doe, John Doe and Janice Doe (not related) started personalising their old trainers together as a hobby after being bored during the covid-19 lockdown. They started doing this to pass time and shared some of their work on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook. Soon they gained followers who gave them positive feedback and started requesting their own personalised trainers and shoes. Since then business has been booming and we deliver custom shoes and trainers all over the UK and to some customers in Europe and the US.

Some of our designs have been featured in prominent publications such as Complex and Time Magazine. We have also appeared on BCC News and CCN.
